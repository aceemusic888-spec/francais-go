Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

FrenchGo ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the FrenchGo application, available on the web and on Google Play.

1. Information We Collect

FrenchGo does not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or any other personal data to use the app.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only, using your browser's local storage:

  • Your selected language (French, English, or Chinese)
  • Your daily streak and XP progress
  • Lessons you have completed
  • Your dark mode preference
  • Whether you have unlocked premium content (via unlock code)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties. FrenchGo does not use advertising networks or tracking technologies.

We use Google Analytics (Google LLC) to collect anonymous usage statistics (pages visited, session duration, device type). This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. Google may process this data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out.

3. In-App Purchases

Premium content can be unlocked via a one-time purchase processed through Gumroad (gumroad.com). Any payment information you provide is handled directly by Gumroad and is subject to Gumroad's Privacy Policy. FrenchGo does not receive or store your payment details.

4. Children's Privacy

FrenchGo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the app and provided information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

5. Offline Use

FrenchGo is designed to work offline. Once installed, the app does not require an internet connection, and no data is transmitted while you use it offline.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
